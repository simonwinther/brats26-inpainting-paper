\section{Results}
\label{sec:results}

The corrected seed-0 training runs and their full 100-case evaluations were still in
progress when this manuscript revision was prepared. We therefore do not promote the
five-case monitoring curves to quantitative results. Those curves are intended for
training diagnostics only and are especially unsuitable for comparison with earlier
runs, which used a different mask-loss normalization and a different five-case
monitoring cohort.

The final ablation table will be populated from the immutable aggregate JSON and
per-case CSV files produced by the shared evaluator. It will report mean $\pm$ sample
standard deviation for SSIM, PSNR, and MSE for the four arms in
Table~\ref{tab:ablation-design}, each evaluated on the exact same 100 case IDs. The
official validation result will be reported separately because its hidden ground truth
and ranking protocol are distinct from the local hold-out.
